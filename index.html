Why work with me?

UX Career Guidance

As a Product Designer with a background in recruitment, I bring immense value to individuals looking to pursue or advance in a career in this field.

​With over 300 successful clients who are now working at companies like HelloFresh, YouTube, Ubisoft, VW, Asana, Hubspot, Amazon, Google and more, I have a proven track record of helping individuals to achieve their career goals. My guidance and support provide a clear path to success in UX design, covering areas such as industry knowledge, skill development, networking, and job placement. By working with me, individuals have the opportunity to take their careers to the next level, resulting in better job opportunities, career growth, and fulfillment.

My services include:

  • Career Assessment: Identify strengths, weaknesses and career goals, creating a tailored roadmap for success.
  • Resume & Portfolio Review: Get in-depth feedback on your resume and portfolio to stand out to potential employers.
  • Interview Preparation: Prepare for job interviews with coaching on common questions, role-playing and feedback.
  • Networking: Build and leverage your professional network to find new opportunities and advance your career.
  • Career Advancement: Navigate job search, salary negotiation, and transitions to new roles with my guidance.
  • Design Challenges: Overcome design obstacles with feedback and problem-solving strategies.

The benefits of working with me:

  • Reach Career Goals: Personalized coaching helps you reach your desired career destination
  • Stay Ahead of Competition: My expertise and experience gives you a competitive edge.
  • Increase Earning Potential: Advance to higher-level roles with increased pay.
  • Build Confidence: Overcome obstacles with my support and advice.
  • Overcome Design Challenges: Gain insights, develop skills, tackle design problems with confidence.
  • Network with Industry Pros: Make valuable connections and expand your professional network.
